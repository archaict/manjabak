//about_us - semicolon_project

Our goal as college student of Pradita Institute is making a pathway for people to learn coding as a language to convey their thought and creative mind. Coding for themost part was never taught as a second language for most people who born in 90’s and before. Coding was such a strange language that we can’t fathom with just see-ing, you learn the language, you learn the code, and then you intuitively write your code in such a way that can be understood in computer language. Think of it like this, you learn English as for the most part second language in asian cultured school, this is mostly true for we who live in Indonesia. We learn the language, the grammar, the conversational skill (that most of us is struggling with) and how to understand the words or sentences that we said. It’s the same for every language, and so it is for the code itself.

//beautiful_mind - coding_your_way

Semicolon Project is a non-profitable branch of coding industry based in Jakarta. Semicolon Project consists of three people from Pradita University. Our goal is to challenge you to go out from your ‘text’ comfort zone and do coding to break your bad habit. Coding is not a therapy that you’ll seek for the first time, it’s really far from it; coding is one of the most logical ‘playgrounds’ for people to learn to organize their own routine, life, and mind. It’s but a journey to immerse yourself in ‘sometimes’ an unfathomable art.
